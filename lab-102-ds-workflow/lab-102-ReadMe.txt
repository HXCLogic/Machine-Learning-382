===============================================================================================
   		A Guide to Choosing the Right Machine Learning Approach
===============================================================================================
Your data holds the key to unlocking powerful insights! 

This guide will help you navigate the exciting journey in the world of Machine Learning (ML) 
and choose the best suitable technique for your specific data type

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
   Roadmap:
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
      	1. Text Data(Think of chatbots for customer service or sentiment analysis)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Lots of Data? Deep Learning is your friend!
  You should invest in a robust data engineering infrastructure to manage it all such as GPU 
  that can significantly boost modeling speed.

* Limited data? No stress! Opt for pre-trained DL models that do not need complex infrastructure
  and GPUs may not be necessary.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	2. Time Series Data (sales forecasting, stock price prediction or inventory optimization)
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Lots of Data? Traditional methods such as XGBoost perform well on TS data.
  Infrastructure - robust - leverage on combination of GPU (for specific models like Transformers 
  or LSTMs) and CPU machines for optimal performance.

* Limited Data? ML might not be the best solution here.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	3. Tabular Data (Product recommendations, fraud detection, marketing campaign 
	   optimization or customer churn)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Lots of Data? Traditional methods often outperform DL in tabular data.
  However, specific tasks like product recommendation, DL can be very effective.
  GPUs may not be beneficial unless when DL is used.
* Limited Data? ML solution might not best suit the problem.
  Modeling can be done by a less specialized team.

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
	4. Image Data (Facial recognition or augmented reality)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Lots of Data? DL shines again! It outperforms traditional methods in image tasks.
  GPUs are a must-have for efficient processing.
* Limited Data? Consider pre-trained DL models first but also question the overal feasibility of 
  a custom ML solution for a CV tasks.
